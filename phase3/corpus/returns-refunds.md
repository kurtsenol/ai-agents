# Returns & Refunds Policy

## 1. Standard Return Window
Customers may return most products within 21 days of the purchase date for a full refund, provided the item is unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by a receipt.

## 2. Exceptions

### 2.1 Final-Sale Items
Clearance merchandise, perishable goods, and personalized products are not eligible for return or refund.

### 2.2 Store #42 Clearance Override
At Store #42, clearance products may be returned within 7 days of purchase, provided the item is unused, in its original packaging, and the customer presents a receipt. This store-specific rule overrides the general exclusion for clearance merchandise in Section 2.1.

## 3. Refund Method
Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 4 business days after the return is approved. If the original payment method is unavailable, the customer may receive store credit instead.

## 4. Receipt Requirements
Customers without a receipt may receive store credit equal to the lowest selling price recorded for the item during the previous 14 days. A valid government-issued photo ID is required for a no-receipt return.
