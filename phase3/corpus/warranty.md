# Product Warranty Policy

## 1. Standard Warranty Periods

Electronics are covered for 18 months. Large appliances are covered for 3 years. Accessories are covered for 90 days.

## 2. Extended Warranty

An optional extended warranty adds 24 months of coverage for a fee equal to 12% of the product's purchase price.

## 3. Claim Process

To make a claim, the customer must provide proof of purchase and a description of the problem, including when the problem began and how the product was being used when it occurred. Approved claims are resolved within 8 business days.

## 4. Exclusions

The warranty does not cover accidental damage, liquid damage, normal cosmetic wear, or repairs performed by unauthorized service providers.
