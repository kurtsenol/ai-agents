# Shipping & Delivery Policy

## 1. Delivery Timeframes

Standard delivery takes 4 business days; express delivery takes 1 business day.

## 2. Shipping Costs

Free standard shipping is available on orders over $85. Otherwise, a flat shipping fee of $6.50 applies.

## 3. Click & Collect

Online orders are ready for in-store pickup within 3 hours and are held for 5 days before being returned to stock.

## 4. Store #42 Same-Day Delivery

Store #42 offers same-day local delivery for orders placed before 14:30 within a 12-km radius, for a fee of $9.50.
