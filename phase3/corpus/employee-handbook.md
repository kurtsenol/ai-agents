# Employee Handbook

## 1. Working Hours & Breaks

A standard shift is 8 hours, with a 45-minute break for shifts over 6 hours.

## 2. Employee Discount

Employees receive a 15% discount, excluding clearance merchandise, gift cards, and alcoholic beverages.

## 3. Dress Code

Employees must wear the company-issued uniform shirt, black trousers or a black skirt of appropriate length, and closed-toe, slip-resistant shoes while working in the store.

## 4. Safety & Incident Reporting

Workplace incidents must be reported to a supervisor within 2 hours using the company's incident reporting form before the end of the employee's shift.
